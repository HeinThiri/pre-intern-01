About YTU

The present Yangon Technological University (Y.T.U)
began as the Department of Engineering in 1923
under the Rangoon (Yangon) University. The
Engineering Department was situated downtown, on
the Bogyoke Aung San street. B.Sc. (Engineering)
program in Civil Engineering began in 1924.
